Findings


  • How does gender compare in workplace promotions?
  • Does pay gap disparity occur in higher education and higher experienced roles?
  • Where are we seeing the biggest gaps between gender compensation?

DE&I awareness is higher than ever. Companies have been openly discussing efforts to grow DE&I initiatives and are spending more on DE&I initiatives than ever before.

Yet with all this progress, we are still seeing equity disparities across gender and other underrepresented groups. Data is valuable in helping us find where the disconnects are but only if you can ask the hard questions and listen with an open mind.
