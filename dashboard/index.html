About Me

Risk Leader | Diversity Champion


Financial Risk Leader, Mother, Lifetime Learner & Advocate.

As a first generation, Kim has taken the unconventional path in most things in life. She is the oldest child and trailblazer in the family. First to graduate college, first to start a career, and first to become a professional leader. Because of her background, Kim is a strong advocate for diversity, equity, and inclusion of underrepresented groups both in the workplace but also in bridging intergenerational divides at home and within the local community.

She finds joy in mentoring and developing leadership skills in others.

At home, Kim enjoys spending time with her family and exploring the world through her children's inquisitive perspectives.
